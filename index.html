To Do List

    How to Use the To-Do List

    1. Adding a Task

  • Click on the input field (where it says "Write here...").
  • Type your task name.
  • Press Enter or click outside the input field, and the task will be added to the list.

    • 2. Marking a Task as Completed

  • Click on the black circular button next to a task.
  • The task will change appearance (if designed that way) to indicate completion.

    • 3. Removing a Task

  • Click the red ‘X’ button next to the task.
  • The task will be removed permanently.
